Our Problem and Solution
The Human Body knows a lot of diseases which may or may not be known to the general public.
Many common diseases are still relatively unknown which comes with some disadvantages.
For example: an idividual might not know of a certain disease and find out to late he/she is ill.
To better inform people about the most common diseases we have designed this interactive tool.

This tool may help someone to learn of a particular disease through the information provided
when hovering over a specific bodypart that the individual might feel to be affacted. He or she
can then go to the external links provided to get further information about the disease in question.







  Our Question  


As noted in the before, there are many diseases unknown to the general public.
We seek to inform people by obtaining data about the most common diseases that occur for some organs.
Unfortunately, there is no statistic on how often a certain disease occurs. We circumvent this
lack of data by opting for the amount of entries per langue for a disease. More language entries
would usually mean that the disease is more widespead and thus more common.

You can find out more about how we acquire such information on our databases page, and you can test out this tool for a
variety of disease clusters on our main homepage.
